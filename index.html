Imagine Dragons-Bones

"Bones" is a song by American pop rock band Imagine Dragons. The song was released through Interscope and Kidinakorner on March 11, 2022, as the lead single from the band's sixth studio album, Mercury .

The Weekend - Starboy

"Starboy" is a song by Canadian singer the Weeknd featuring French electronic duo Daft Punk. It was released on September 21, 2016, through XO and Republic Records, as the lead single from his third studio album of the same name ."Starboy" topped the charts in countries such as Canada, France, Netherlands, New Zealand and Sweden.
