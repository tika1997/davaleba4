"Love Theory" by Kirk Franklin is a song about the power of God's love for his people. It focuses on the incredible grace and mercy of God, who loves and accepts us in spite of our shortcomings and mistakes. The chorus expresses the desire of the narrator to honor and make God proud. Throughout the song, Kirk Franklin emphasizes that God's love is the only true and lasting one, and encourages us to be grateful for it and strive to reciprocate it. Ultimately, the song is a reminder to always remember that God is our savior and that his love is unconditional and unending.
